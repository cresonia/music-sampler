One Direction

One Direction is an English-Irish boy band composed of Niall Horan, Liam Payne, Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson and formerly Zayn Malik. The band primarily focuses on upbeat, pop music. However, with recent albums they've been venturing into more mature, darker sounds and content. So from what initially drew its appeal from happy, high-energy and electric beats, their newer content reveals a more raw version of the human condition - of deceit, of selfishness and of pleasure. The band may have began with innocence, but popularity continues because of sheer guilty pleasure. It remains to be seen how long this will last.
